El Pollo Loco

Pepe, the cheerful salsa chef, loves creating salsa concoctions in his small town. One day, his village was invaded by a horde of giant chickens. Bravely, Pepe takes his salsa bottles and confronts the feathered intruders. To defeat the Giant Chicken, Pepe must be clever enough to jump on the heads of small chickens, collecting extra salsa bottles along the way. Only with enough salsa bottles can Pepe defeat the Giant Chicken and free his village from the chicken menace. With Pepe leading the way, players must utilize his jumping skills and salsa know-how to save the village and celebrate the ultimate salsa party.
